About

About Me

I specialise in planning and conducting epidemiological research, utilising digital data‑collection platforms and applying advanced analytics in R and Stata to generate actionable insights. I am passionate about open science; I share reproducible code on GitHub and advocate transparent, version-controlled workflows. I also leverage generative AI to accelerate literature reviews, refine code, and build automated workflows. Comfortable in multidisciplinary teams, I focus on translating reliable data into practical improvements for public health.
